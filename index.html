How it works

Three steps between "we should hang out" and a date in everyone's calendar.

1

Create the pin

A title and 2–6 date options. Done in 30 seconds, no sign-up — you get one shareable link.

2

Drop it in the chat

Everyone taps works / maybe / can't straight from the browser. You watch the votes come in live.

3

Pin the winner

Lock the best date, share the confirmation card back into the chat, and everyone adds it to their calendar in one tap.

The date announces itself.

When you pin the winning date, Pinaday generates a share card for the group chat or your story — so nobody can claim they didn't know. That's the whole point: the plan doesn't leak away in scroll-back.

Frequently asked questions

Is Pinaday free?

Yes. Creating pins, voting and sharing is completely free, without ads. You never need an account.

Do my friends need to install anything?

No. The link opens straight in the browser and voting takes about 5 seconds — on iPhone and Android alike.

How is it different from Doodle?

No accounts, no ads, no apps. Everything works through one link, built for the group chat on your phone.

What happens to my data?

Pins expire automatically after 30 days and only people with the link can see them. Hosted in the EU.
